Bentota

Bentota, a graphic littoral city nestled along the southwest seacoast of Sri Lanka, is famed for its pristine strands, lush verdure, and abundant wildlife. One of its notable lodestones is the Bentota Hatchery, a sanctuary for ocean turtles. The Bentota Hatchery serves as a vital conservation center, devoted to guarding and nurturing these majestic brutes. Callers can witness the remarkable trip of ocean turtles, from the moment they lay their eggs on the flaxen props to the hatching of lovable baby turtles. The hatchery plays a pivotal part in educating the public about the significance of conserving these risked species and raising mindfulness about the pitfalls they face in the wild. It offers a unique occasion for callers to interact with the turtles, learn about their life cycle, and indeed share in the release of hatchlings into the vast Indian Ocean. The Bentota Hatchery isn't only a witching magnet but also a testament to the sweats made to guard the biodiversity and natural heritage of Sri Lanka's littoral ecosystems.
